Project Overview

McCronalds ($MCD) is a community-driven meme token built on the Cronos blockchain, designed to bring fun, utility, and decentralized finance opportunities together. Our mission is to create a vibrant ecosystem where holders can enjoy both entertainment and value.

Tokenomics

The total supply of $MCD is capped at 1,000,000,000 tokens to ensure scarcity and value retention. We have implemented a deflationary mechanism where a small percentage of every transaction is burned, reducing supply over time and rewarding long-term holders.

Roadmap

Our roadmap includes launching community events, listing on major decentralized exchanges, developing partnerships, and rolling out McCronalds merchandise. Future plans involve integrating NFT collaborations and building a McCronalds-themed game.

Security & Transparency

The McCronalds smart contract has been audited by reputable third-party security firms to ensure safety. We are committed to transparency and regular updates to our community through official channels.

Community & Governance

We believe in the power of community and plan to introduce governance tokens allowing holders to vote on important project decisions, ensuring McCronalds evolves with its users.
